Frequently Asked Questions

What is the validity of this certification?

The Thriving Workplace certification remains valid for up to 12 months from the date of the previous certification.

How long does the certification process take?

The certification process typically spans a maximum of 50 days, comprising:
18-20 days for survey launch and completion
20-30 days for report generation and Certification.

What are the evaluation criteria?

Certification is granted when our rigorous benchmarks are met:
•⁠  ⁠60% workforce participation in the survey
•⁠  ⁠Attainment of a score of 75% or higher across at least 6 key Levers.

How confidential is the survey?

The Thriving Workplace survey guarantees full anonymity, ensuring that employers have no visiblity or access to individual responses, and employees are not identifiable by their feedback.

Are the surveys customizable?

Thriving Workplace utilizes a scientifically proven framework for assessment and certification, thus the surveys are not customizable.

Do you offer support for multiple languages?

Our surveys are primarily available in English (UK), with additional options in two Indian languages: Tamil and Hindi.

Why will my employees participate in this survey?

Employees are encouraged to participate as it provides them with an opportunity to voice their opinions, acknowledge organizational efforts, suggest new initiatives, and receive personalized reports about themselves for actionable insights.

What will my organization gain from participating in this survey?

Participating organizations receive both physical and digital certificates, along with a digital badge for sharing across various platforms including social media, internal communications, and for external PR exercises.

Can I receive customized reports?

Yes, customized reports tailored to your specific needs and requirements will be provided upon request prior to survey launch.
